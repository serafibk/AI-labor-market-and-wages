Using the Mann-Whitney U test on the empiricial distribution of W_F in order to test if one survival function of the distribution (opposite of CDF) stochastically dominates another.

null hypothesis: setting i and j result in the same distirbution of average final wage values
alternative hypothesis: setting j results in a wage distribution that is stochastically greater than that of setting i

interpretation:
Comparison of setting i with setting j => if test(setting j, setting i, alternative="less") results in p<0.05 => setting i results in a wage distribution that is (significantly) stochastically greater than that of setting j
